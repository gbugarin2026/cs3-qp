ABOUT THE AUTHOR

"Sarili ay mahalaga kahit pa anong tingin nila. Tanggap kita."
- 'Di Ka Sayang, Ben&Ben

Hi. I'm Gwyn Bugarin of 9 - Pinatubo. I've been a Ben&Ben fan since 2018, and not once did I ever regret listening to a song of a band that was once new to me.

Alongside Ben&Ben, I also listen to Eraserheads, Itchyworms, Moonstar88, and OPM artists similar to them. I honestly don't see myself not listening to our country's music. It kind of just grew on me.
